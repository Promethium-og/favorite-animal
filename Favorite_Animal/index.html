Immortal Jellyfish

About Them

Turritopsis dohrnii, also known as the immortal jellyfish, is a
species of small, biologically immortal jellyfish[2][3] found worldwide
in temperate to tropic waters. It is one of the few known cases
of animals capable of reverting completely to a sexually immature,
colonial stage after having reached sexual maturity as a solitary
individual. Others include the jellyfish Laodicea undulata[4] and
species of the genus Aurelia.[5]
